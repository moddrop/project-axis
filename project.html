Wind Energy Vs Sun Energy

Back then fossil fuels were used as our main source of energy, rather than clean energies such as wind and sun energy. So now we are seeing which is better in certain conditions. Our experiment was powering a wind turbine and solar panel to power an LED to see which one has better output. It showed that both energies actually were similar.

In the future, we will be using more clean energy and less fossil fuels. We will be using more wind and sun energy to power our homes and cars. We will be using more wind turbines and solar panels to power our homes and cars. We will be using more clean energy to power our homes and cars.

Comparing Air Quality in the past, present, future

Our project is about how humans affect and cause bad air quality in the past, present, and future. We have made a device that detects and measures the air quality. The device beeps whenever the air quality increases severely. We are showing examples of how bad the air quality is and was in the past, present, and future.

Our group wants to see how human activity causes bad air quality, and by using our device and future perspectives, we could possibly help people come up with ideas to improve the air quality

SoundView Smart Glasses

Globally, at least 2.2 billion people have a near or distance vision impairment. In at least 1 billion of these, vision impairment could have been prevented or is yet to be addressed. That leaves roughly 1.1-2.2 billion people with vision problems not easily solved by glasses. They need to find a better way to live. I believe we have found a solution. This is SoundView. As the name suggests, these glasses provide an accurate description of the world around them. With a microcontroller, a few wires, and a speaker, something great can be made.

SoundView works by using a microcontroller to process the data from the camera. The camera detects the objects around the user and sends that information to the microcontroller. The microcontroller then processes that information and sends it to the speaker, which provides an accurate description of the world around them.
