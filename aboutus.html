"Building the future"

"We’re two friends building projects and learning to code together. I’m 13 and I’ve made smart glasses and a plant monitoring project using Arduino. Binbin is 12, and he built a CO₂ and smoke detector system. We both really like working with electronics and turning ideas into real things."

"Right now, we’re also developing this website together on CodePen. We’re learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as we go, testing ideas, breaking stuff, and fixing it. This site is basically our digital workshop where we share our projects and show what we’re working on next."
