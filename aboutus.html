Caleb Ethan

Caleb: "Hi, Caleb here. Ever since I was a kid, I had always had a keen interest in programming. I started with Python, then HTML, then CSS. That was when I found out about C Embedded Systems programming. I started experimenting. I began learning about API usage, basic digital pin control and analog reads. And here I am now founding a new initiative. Introducing Project Axis. We are creating a business meant to help the blind. I believe this to be a humanitarian cause, and I hope to help people in the future."

Binbin Zhao

Binbin: "Hello, I'm Binbin, I started by understanding basic programming concepts using Scratch, then I made my way to HTML/CSS for its simplicity. I later learned Javascript and when I saw Caleb's ideas I explored on how microntrollers work and the C programming language, specifically embedded in Arduino IDE. In Project AXIS, I handle front-end development in the website, and a little bit of back-end. I am exited to see our project make a positive impact for blind users in the future."
