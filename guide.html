⊞ Dashboard
Stats
The four cards at the top count all log entries, entries from the past 7 days, total comments, and how many projects have entries. They update in real time every second.
Issues panel
Shows only Issue-type entries grouped by project. Use the date filter (All / Today / This Week / This Month) to narrow the view. Click any entry to jump to that project's log. Resolved issues show a ✓ instead of ⚠.
Upcoming Goals
Lists all incomplete goals across every project, sorted by due date. Click the circle button to mark a goal complete — it fades out immediately and the count updates. The panel refreshes every 30 seconds.
The dashboard auto-refreshes entries every second. You don't need to reload the page.
◈ Projects
Grid view
Each card shows the project's progress bar, status, lead, and tag. The top colour strip matches the project colour. Use the All / Active / Review / Complete filter buttons to narrow the list.
Progress bar
Progress is calculated automatically — goals take priority: if a project has goals, the bar shows goals completed ÷ total goals. If no goals exist but the project has start and end dates, the bar shows time elapsed. Otherwise the stored percentage is used. The bar turns red if overdue with no goals.
New project
Click + New Project. Fill in name, description, icon (any emoji), colour, status, category, lead name, and optional start / deadline dates. Progress starts at 0 and is driven by goals you add later.
Edit / Delete
Hover a project card — two buttons appear in the top-right corner: ✎ Edit (update name, dates, status, lead) and ✕ Delete (permanently removes the project and all its entries, comments, and goals).
Log view
Click a project card to open its log. You'll see the project bar with live progress, the Goals & Timeline panel, and the entry list. Use the type filter or search box to find specific entries.
◌ Log Entries
Types
Log — general note. Update — progress report. Milestone — major achievement. Issue — a problem that needs solving (appears in the dashboard Issues panel and can be resolved).
Create
Click + New Entry while viewing a project log. Choose a type, write a title (required), and optionally add details. Press Enter or click Save.
Edit / Delete
Hover an entry card to reveal the Edit and Delete buttons. Deleting an entry also removes all its comments. Deletions take effect instantly in the UI.
Comments
Click the 💬 N comments button on any entry to expand the comments section. Type a reply and press Enter or click Post. You can delete your own comments; you cannot delete other people's comments.
⚠ Issues
Reporting
Create a log entry and set the type to Issue. It will immediately appear in the dashboard Issues panel and be flagged with ⚠.
Resolving
Hover an Issue entry and click Resolve. The badge changes to ✓ RESOLVED instantly. Click Unresolve to revert. Resolved issues are visible to all team members and persist across sessions.
The dashboard only shows issues, not regular log entries. Use the Projects page to see all entry types.
★ Goals & Timeline
Adding
Open a project log and click + Add Goal. Enter a goal title and an optional due date, then click Add. Goals are shared — all team members see them.
Completing
Click the ○ circle next to a goal to mark it complete. It instantly shows ✓ and the project's progress bar updates without a page reload. Click again to mark it incomplete.
Due dates
Goals due today or overdue are highlighted in red. Goals due within 7 days are shown in amber. The progress bar on overdue projects with no goals turns red.
Deleting
Hover a goal row and click the ✕ that appears on the right. The goal disappears immediately and the progress bar recalculates.
Goals drive the progress bar. If you want accurate progress tracking, add goals with due dates rather than editing the percentage manually.
💬 Direct Messages
Opening
Click the 💬 button in the top-right corner of any page. A panel slides in from the right showing all team members. Click a name to open the conversation.
Sending
Type a message and press Enter or click Send. Messages appear instantly without waiting for a server response. The conversation refreshes every 2 seconds while open.
Unread badge
A red badge on the 💬 button shows how many unread messages you have across all conversations. Opening a conversation marks those messages as read. The count updates every 15 seconds.
🔔 Notifications
Bell
The 🔔 button in the top bar shows how many new log entries have appeared since you last checked. Click it to see the 6 most recent entries across all projects. New entries are marked with an orange dot.
Mark all read
Click Mark all read inside the bell dropdown. This syncs across your devices — if you mark read on your phone, it clears on your laptop within 15 seconds.
Per-account
Notification state is stored per account. Each employee has an independent notification counter — clearing yours doesn't affect your teammates.
⊛ Account & Security
Session
Sessions last 8 hours from login, stored in an HttpOnly cookie. After 8 hours you'll be redirected to the login page automatically.
Logout
Click Logout in the top-right corner. This immediately invalidates your session on the server — you can't reuse the same cookie even from another device.
Passwords
Passwords are hashed with PBKDF2-SHA256 and stored server-side. To change a password, ask an admin to regenerate the hash and redeploy the Worker.
Rate limiting
After 5 failed login attempts from the same IP address, the account is locked for 5 minutes. This is per-IP, not per-account.
The Settings page (⊛) shows your active sessions and lets you revoke them remotely.
